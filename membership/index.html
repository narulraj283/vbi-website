Membership Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about VBI membership

How does the free trial work?

+

All paid membership tiers include a 3-month free trial. You'll get full access to all tier benefits with no credit card required to start. After the trial period, your membership will convert to the regular monthly or annual rate unless you cancel. You can cancel anytime, even during the trial—no questions asked, no early termination fees.

Can I switch membership tiers anytime?

+

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your membership tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll only pay the difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the change will apply at your next renewal date. No penalties or fees apply to changes.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal. Payments are processed securely through Stripe, and you can update your payment method anytime in your account settings. Invoices are provided for every transaction for your records.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

+

Yes, we offer significant savings on annual billing. Essentials saves you $189/year, Growth saves you $489/year, and Elite saves you $1,189/year compared to monthly billing. The savings are displayed on the membership page and automatically applied when you choose annual billing during checkout.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

+

Yes. You can cancel your membership anytime through your account settings. If you cancel during a free trial, no charge will apply. If you cancel after your trial, the cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. No refunds are issued for partial months, but you'll have access to your membership through the end date.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

+

Your profile and any data you've created in the community will remain accessible for 30 days after cancellation, allowing you to download or export your information. After 30 days, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted. You can request a full data export before canceling by contacting our support team.
