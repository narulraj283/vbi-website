- For index.html (different format with title attributes):
  - `<a href="#" title="Facebook" aria-label="Facebook">f</a>` -> `<a href="{FACEBOOK_URL}" title="Facebook" aria-label="Facebook" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">f</a>`
  - `<a href="#" title="LinkedIn" aria-label="LinkedIn">in</a>` -> `<a href="{LINKEDIN_URL}" title="LinkedIn" aria-label="LinkedIn" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">in</a>`
