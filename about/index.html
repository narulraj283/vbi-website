About Veterinary Business Institute

Transforming the veterinary industry through education, community, and resources

Our Mission

To empower veterinary practice owners and managers with practical business education, access to proven resources, and connection to a supportive community.

We believe that veterinary professionals should succeed in business as much as they do in medicine. By providing education, tools, and community, we help practices thrive.

Our Vision

A world where every veterinary practice owner has the knowledge, tools, and support needed to build a profitable, sustainable, and fulfilling business.

We envision a veterinary industry where business success doesn't require an MBA, where best practices are shared freely, and where every practice can reach its potential.

Meet the Founder

👨‍💼

Naren Arulrajah

Founder & CEO, VBI

Naren Arulrajah is the Founder and CEO of Veterinary Business Institute and the founding team at Ekwa Marketing, a digital marketing agency focused exclusively on veterinary practices.

With over a decade of experience in veterinary marketing and business strategy, Naren has helped hundreds of practices increase revenue, improve team engagement, and achieve their business goals.

Frustrated by the lack of practical business education available to veterinary professionals, Naren founded VBI to democratize veterinary business knowledge and create a supportive community for practice owners and managers.

When he's not obsessing over practice metrics, Naren enjoys reading, traveling, and supporting the veterinary community through education and mentorship.

Our Connection to Ekwa Marketing

VBI was founded by the team at Ekwa Marketing, a digital marketing agency dedicated exclusively to veterinary practices. This connection is important to understand about our mission.

After working with hundreds of veterinary practices, the Ekwa team realized that many practices were struggling not just with marketing, but with fundamental business challenges: pricing, operations, team management, and profitability.

VBI was created to address these broader business challenges. While Ekwa Marketing continues to provide specialized digital marketing services, VBI provides comprehensive business education and resources.

Why we're transparent about this relationship:

We believe in complete transparency. VBI is an independent organization, but we benefit from the deep industry expertise of the Ekwa team. This benefits our members through better content, resources, and community. At the same time, VBI is designed to help all practices succeed, not just those using Ekwa's marketing services.
