Our Event Formats

Designed to fit your schedule and learning style

🎓

Workshops

60-90 minutes | Interactive | Monthly

Focused sessions on specific practice challenges. Expert-led with Q&A, practical takeaways, and downloadable resources. Perfect for busy owners who want actionable insights fast.

📚

Masterclasses

60+ minutes | Deep Dive | On-Demand

Comprehensive courses on major topics like financial management, practice valuation, growth strategy, and leadership. Includes workbooks, templates, and lifetime access. Learn at your own pace.

🚀

Partner Demo Days

2 hours | Live Demos | Quarterly

Discover innovative solutions from our vetted partners. See live software demos, ask questions directly with experts, and access exclusive Q3-Q4 discounts. Virtual format with chat support.

🤝

Regional Meetups

2 hours | Networking | Quarterly

In-person gatherings for practice owners in your region. Build relationships with peers facing similar challenges. Casual, friendly format with light refreshments. Open to Growth & Elite members.

Annual Summit

3 days | Comprehensive | October

The premier event for independent veterinary practice leaders. Keynotes, workshops, networking dinners, partner showcase, and exclusive mastermind groups. The largest gathering of practice owners.

Special Events

Varies | Exclusive | Throughout the Year

Occasional specialized events including industry trend briefings, special guest speakers, roundtables on current topics, and Elite member-only gatherings. Announced in advance to members.
