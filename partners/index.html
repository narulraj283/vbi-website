Partnership Questions

How are leads and member connections handled?

+

Partners receive direct marketplace listings where members can discover your solutions and special offers. For higher-tier partnerships, we provide quarterly analytics showing member engagement and demo requests. All lead follow-up is handled directly between your team and interested members—we facilitate the introduction but don't restrict communication.

What's included in the "co-creation" opportunities?

+

Co-creation varies by partnership tier. Typically, this means hosting webinars together, creating case studies featuring your solutions, developing joint resources, or designing special programs for our members. Your expertise combined with our platform = valuable content that drives both brand awareness and member value.

Can we customize our partnership deal or package?

+

Absolutely. The tiers above are guidelines. For Founding and Premier-level partners, we can customize elements to match your specific goals, target audience preferences, and marketing calendar. Reach out to discuss what would work best for your organization.

How long is a partnership commitment?

+

Standard partnerships run on an annual basis, with renewal discussed 90 days before expiration. We're flexible for strong partnerships and can discuss multi-year commitments. Community-tier partnerships can be as short as 6 months if preferred.
