Common Questions

Quick answers to help you find what you need

How do I cancel my membership?

+

You can cancel anytime through your account settings in the VBI member portal. Just log in, go to Account > Billing, and select "Cancel Membership." You'll have access through the end of your current billing cycle. No penalties or early termination fees apply.

How do I reset my password?

+

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email within a few minutes. Follow the link to create a new password. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or contact us for help.

How long does the free trial last?

+

Paid membership tiers (Essentials, Growth, and Elite) include a 3-month free trial. You'll get full access to all tier benefits with no credit card required to start. After the trial ends, your membership converts to the regular monthly or annual rate unless you cancel.

Who can I speak with about partnership?

+

Partnership inquiries are handled by our Partnership team. You can reach out via the "Become a Partner" form on our Partners page, or email hello@veterinarybusinessinstitute.com with "Partnership Inquiry" in the subject line. We'll connect you with the right person within 2-3 business days.

Can I be a guest on the podcast?

+

We're always looking for interesting guests—practice owners with unique stories, industry experts, and partners with valuable insights. Submit your interest using the contact form and select "Podcast Guest Request" as the subject, or email hello@veterinarybusinessinstitute.com with podcast details about you or your company.

Do you offer refunds?

+

Membership is non-refundable but cancellable anytime. If you cancel during a free trial, no charge applies. If you cancel after the trial, you'll have access through the end of your current billing cycle but won't receive a refund for the remaining time. Contact us if you have special circumstances—we're here to help.

We're Here to Help
